Privacy-First Commitment: Smart Form Assistant is designed with your privacy as the absolute top priority. Your personal information never leaves your browser, and we have no access to any of your data.

Information We Do NOT Collect

Smart Form Assistant operates with a strict no-data-collection policy:

  • We do not collect any personal information
  • We do not track your browsing habits
  • We do not monitor your form submissions
  • We do not store any data on external servers
  • We do not use analytics or tracking scripts
  • We do not share data with third parties

How Your Data is Stored

Local Storage Only

All your information is stored exclusively in your browser using Chrome's local storage APIs. This means:

  • Data remains on your device at all times
  • No information is transmitted over the internet
  • Extension works completely offline
  • You have complete control over your data

What Gets Stored Locally

The extension stores the following information in your browser:

  • Profile Information: Personal details you choose to save (name, email, address, etc.)
  • Form Templates: Structure patterns of forms you've used (without sensitive data)
  • Settings: Your extension preferences and configuration
  • Usage Statistics: Basic counts for extension features (stored locally only)

Data Security

We implement several security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: All stored data is encrypted using browser security standards
  • Isolation: Data is isolated to the extension and not accessible by websites
  • No Network Access: Extension doesn't communicate with external servers
  • User Control: You can view, edit, export, or delete your data anytime

Permissions Explained

The extension requests the following permissions for essential functionality:

Storage Permission

Used to save your profile and form templates locally in your browser. No data is sent externally.

Active Tab Permission

Allows the extension to detect forms on the current webpage you're viewing. This is necessary for auto-fill functionality.

Scripting Permission

Enables the extension to analyze form structures and provide auto-fill suggestions. Scripts only run locally.

Host Permissions

Required to access form fields on all websites for auto-filling. No data from these sites is collected or transmitted.

Your Control Over Data

Data Management

You have complete control over your information:

  • View: Access all stored data through the profile interface
  • Edit: Modify or update any saved information
  • Export: Download your data as a JSON file for backup
  • Import: Restore data from a previously exported file
  • Delete: Clear all stored data with one click

Uninstallation

When you uninstall the extension, all stored data is automatically removed from your browser. No traces are left behind.

Website Interaction

Form Detection

The extension analyzes webpage forms to provide auto-fill functionality. This analysis happens entirely in your browser and no form data is transmitted externally.

Auto-Fill Process

When auto-filling forms, the extension uses only your locally stored profile information. It does not access or transmit any data from the forms themselves.

Third-Party Services

Smart Form Assistant does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or external APIs. The extension operates in complete isolation.

Changes to Privacy Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, we will:

  • Notify users through the extension interface
  • Clearly highlight any changes
  • Maintain our commitment to privacy-first design
  • Never introduce data collection without explicit consent

Contact Information

For privacy-related questions or concerns, you can:

  • Review the extension's open-source code
  • Check browser console logs for transparency
  • Verify no network requests are made by the extension
Verification: You can independently verify our privacy claims by monitoring your browser's network activity while using the extension. You'll see no outgoing requests or data transmission.

Compliance

This extension is designed to comply with:

  • Chrome Web Store Privacy Requirements
  • GDPR Privacy Regulations
  • CCPA Privacy Standards
  • General Privacy Best Practices
